# Company Profile: Synapse AI

## 1. Overview
- **Elevator Pitch:** Synapse AI is the world's first autonomous GTM platform that replaces fragmented marketing and sales tools with a unified 17-agent ecosystem that researches, qualifies, and closes enterprise deals.
- **Mission:** To eliminate GTM friction by enabling every enterprise to deploy a high-performance, autonomous sales and marketing organization in minutes.
- **Core Problem Solved:** High CAC, lead response lag, and fragmented data across sales/marketing stacks that prevent scalable growth.

## 2. Product/Service Details
- **Main Offering:** Synapse Agentic GTM Platform
- **Key Features:**
  - **Autonomous 17-Agent Core:** A complete organizational structure (CMO to CSM) that works 24/7.
  - **A2A Orchestrator:** Proprietary engine that allows agents to hand off context and tasks seamlessly.
  - **Secure Tool Gateway:** Pre-built, secure integrations with HubSpot, Apollo, Slack, and Notion via MCP.
  - **SAIF Security Layer:** Enterprise-grade prompt injection and data exfiltration protection.
- **Differentiation:** Unlike standard automation (Zapier/Make), Synapse uses "thinking" agents that reason over context and adjust tactics in real-time.

## 3. Pricing & Packaging
- **Tiers:**
  - **Starter:** $2,500/mo (3 core agents, 1 seat).
  - **Growth:** $7,500/mo (10 agents, 5 seats, full CRM integration).
  - **Enterprise:** Custom (Full 17-agent ecosystem, custom MCP integrations, 24/7 support).
- **Packaging Logic:** Per-agent deployment with unlimited task volume.

## 4. Brand & Messaging
- **Voice/Tone:** Professional, Bold, Visionary, Data-Driven.
- **Key Narratives:** "The End of the Fragmented GTM Stack," "Hire Your First Autonomous SDR Team Today," "Scale to $100M ARR with 1/10th the Headcount."

## 5. Strategic Context
- **Current Goals:** Rapid expansion into US-based Fintech and Healthtech markets.
- **Competitive Landscape:** Competes with traditional SDR agencies, point-solution automation (Outreach/Salesloft), and legacy CRM consultancies. Synapse wins on total cost of ownership and speed to lead.
