## Part 5: ICP Validation Checklist

Before locking your ICP, confirm:

- [ ] You have interviewed at least 10 existing customers who match this profile
- [ ] Your best-fit customers (lowest churn, highest NPS, highest expansion) all fall within this definition
- [ ] Sales and Marketing agree on the definition
- [ ] The ICP is specific enough to use as a filter (not "any company that could benefit")
- [ ] You have identified at least 3 intent signals you can track or buy data on
