# ICP & Personas: Synapse AI

## 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Target Industries:** B2B SaaS, Fintech, Cybersecurity, Healthtech.
- **Company Size:** 200-2,000 employees, Series B to IPO stages, $20M-$250M ARR.
- **Geographic Focus:** North America, UK, and DACH region.
- **Technographic Requirements:** Uses HubSpot or Salesforce, heavy Slack users, utilizes Apollo or Clay for data.

## 2. Key Personas
### Persona 1: VP of Sales / CRO
- **Pain Points:** Missed revenue targets, high SDR turnover, poor lead-to-opportunity conversion rates.
- **Motivations:** Scaling pipeline predictably, reducing CAC, maximizing AE quota attainment.
- **Common Objections:** "Will this replace my team?" "How do I trust the AI output?" "Implementation seems complex."
- **Value Proposition:** Autonomous agents handle the high-volume 'grunt work' of prospecting, allowing AEs to focus 100% on closing.

### Persona 2: Head of RevOps / Sales Ops
- **Pain Points:** Fragmented data, complex automation workflows that constantly break, lack of visibility into multi-touch attribution.
- **Motivations:** Clean data, efficient lead routing, unified GTM tech stack.
- **Common Objections:** "Does it integrate with our custom CRM fields?" "Who owns the maintenance of the agents?"
- **Value Proposition:** A single, unified platform that replaces 5-10 separate tools and handles lead routing automatically.

## 3. Buying Triggers
- **Trigger 1:** New CRO hire tasked with rapid scaling.
- **Trigger 2:** Company raises a new funding round (Series B+).
- **Trigger 3:** Recent layoff or restructuring of the SDR/BDR team.
- **Trigger 4:** Hiring for a "Director of GTM Innovation" or similar forward-thinking roles.
